La Bamba Frisbee Club

About

Welcome to La Bamba Frisbee Club! We’re thrilled to host the Gothrow 2026 ultimate tournament in Gothenburg. Expect a full weekend of spirited games, music, and community.

Key details

  • Dates: 17–18 January 2026
  • Venue: Lundbystrandshallen, Gothenburg (map)
  • Divisions: Mixed (5v5)
  • Format: Swiss-draw into brackets (subject to entries)

Fees & deadlines

  • Team fee: 3500 SEK
  • Player fee: 200 SEK
  • Registration closes: 15 December 2025
  • Payment: Details sent upon acceptance

What’s included

  • Water stations & first aid
  • Saturday social & photo booth

Contact

  • Email: labambafrisbee@gmail.com
  • Instagram: @labambaultimate
  • Questions about teams? Use the Registration tab.

We value Spirit of the Game. All experience levels are welcome—please bring your best spirit and great vibes.
